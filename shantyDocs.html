LEARN SHANTY

SHANTY is a syntax to make writing e-books easier. With minimal markup, writing can be easily converted to NDH E-Reader format.

SHANTY is platform-independent. You may write in any text editor of your choosing, or in VVSTE (Very Very Simple Text Editor).

The NDH E-Reader is a simple, lightweight e-reader platform, with features including dark mode, bookmarking, illustrations, audiobooks, and cover art. It is browser-based, taking up minimal bandwidth, and is mobile-responsive.

Shanty makes it a snap to convert your text files into an HTML file that fits the format of the NDH E-Reader. You don't need to spend an hour getting all the HTML tags into your story.

SYNTAX RULES

METADATA

At the beginning of your document, you should include all of the necessary metadata.

  • !!TITLE [Put title here]
  • !!SUBTITLE
  • !!CREDIT
  • !!AUTHOR
  • !!ILLUSTRATOR
  • !!COVERART

Strictly speaking, none of this metadata is required for the NDH E-Reader format.

At the end of your document, you may want to include information on where and when your content was written.

  • !!LOCATION [Whatever you want]
  • !!DATE [YYYY-MM-DD]

Some people prefer MM-DD-YYYY formats… I for one care less for them.

CHAPTERS

"#" is used for chapter titles, and "## is used for chapter subtitles.

  • #Chapter One
  • ##The Morning Shift

VVSTE's text-parsing program will look for these codes, and use them to build your E-Books Table of Contents.

ADDING CONTENT

"@@" is used for adding special content to your document.

COVER ART

  • @@COVERIMG [file path]

ILLUSTRATIONS

The NDH E-Reader can support an indefinite number of illustrations.

  • @@ILLO :small [ilustration thumbnail filepath] :large [illustration filepath] :buy [link to web page where illustration can be purchased] :orientation [landscape or portrait] :caption [text]
