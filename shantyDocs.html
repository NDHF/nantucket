SHANTY is a syntax to make writing e-books easier. With minimal markup, writing can be easily converted to NDH E-Reader format.

SHANTY is platform-independent. You may write in any text editor of your choosing, or in VVSTE (Very Very Simple Text Editor).

The NDH E-Reader is a simple, lightweight e-reader platform, with features including dark mode, bookmarking, illustrations, audiobooks, and cover art. It is browser-based, taking up minimal bandwidth, and is mobile-responsive.

Shanty makes it a snap to convert your text files into an HTML file that fits the format of the NDH E-Reader. You don't need to spend an hour getting all the HTML tags into your story.

SYNTAX RULES

METADATA

At the beginning of your document, you should include all of the necessary metadata.

  • !!TITLE [Put title here]
  • !!SUBTITLE
  • !!CREDIT
  • !!AUTHOR
  • !!ILLUSTRATOR
  • !!COVERART
  • !!AUDIO

If you would like to include audio files with your e-book, include the !!AUDIO notice in your metadata.

Users will access the audio from the e-reader menu, by clicking on the cassette icon:

This will take users to the audio section.

!!AUDIO :background [source of background image] :copyright [recording copyright information]

  • The :background notice specifies the image you'd like for the background of the audio section. The image is stylistically altered to emphasize the foreground.
    • If no background is specified, the e-reader will default to the seashell color.
  • The :copyright notice allows you to specify the copyright status for the audio file.
  • :copyright MUST come after :background

Strictly speaking, none of this metadata is required for the NDH E-Reader format.

At the end of your document, you may want to include information on where and when your content was written.

  • !!LOCATION [Whatever you want]
  • !!DATE [YYYY-MM-DD]

Some people prefer MM-DD-YYYY formats… I for one care less for them.

CHAPTERS

"#" is used for chapter titles, and "##'' is used for chapter subtitles.

  • #Chapter One
  • ##The Morning Shift

VVSTE's text-parsing program will look for these codes, and use them to build your e-book's Table of Contents.

The NDH E-Reader uses three tables of contents: a drop-down list accessed from the menu for desktop users, a full page for mobile users, and one displayed within the page layout for accessibility.

ADDING CONTENT

"@@" is used for adding special content to your document.

COVER ART

  • @@COVERIMG [file path]

ILLUSTRATIONS

The NDH E-Reader can support an indefinite number of illustrations.

@@ILLO :small [illustration thumbnail filepath] :large [illustration filepath] :buy [link to web page where illustration can be purchased] :orientation [landscape or portrait] :caption [text]

:caption MUST come last.

AUDIO FILES

The NDH E-Reader supports an indefinite number of audio files.

@@AUDIO :source [audio filepath] :title [name for audio file]

:title MUST come last.
